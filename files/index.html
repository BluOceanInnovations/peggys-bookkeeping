Our story

Started by Peggy. Run by her daughter. Trusted for generations.

Peggy Moore founded this firm in Mesquite in 1977. When she passed in 1990, her daughter Sharon — known around town as the "magic tax lady" — took the reins and never looked back. Today Sharon runs the practice alongside Enrolled Agent Anita Molidina and a team that treats your books like their own.

We're affordable, experienced, and friendly — and proud of all three. We've helped countless businesses lower their tax bill and become more profitable, and helped families keep every deduction they've earned. No matter who we're working with, the goal is the same: a long-term relationship built on plain answers and real results.
